Promotions & Holidays

Is the store running a current promotional campaign on this day? Promotions are one of the strongest predictors of higher sales — they typically increase revenue by 15–25%.

Is today a public or state holiday? Stores are often closed or have reduced hours on public holidays, which significantly impacts sales. Select the type if applicable.
